Today's Problem

Do you ever see countries work together build each other up? You have probably seen countries help each other, but not in a big scale. You will mostly see countries just minding their own buisness. But what if we could give them each other attentions? Could it make a difference in the world by world cooperation? Would it bring both the world leaders and the population closer? Let's say, that we start with something small, but something very effective. And with this a little push, building world cooperation.

The Solution

The solution shouldn't be difficult to accomplish, but should be easy to access. We also know how greedy countries can be, so it shouldn't be non profitable. Our solution is to make a common world recourse bank. This is where countries can put in recourses they for example have alot of. And other contries that for example doesn't have alot of exactly those recourses, take it. And if the country aren't willing to give alot of the recourses, maybe they can trade instead. What I am trying to say, is that we create a system where countries help each other by providing recourses, which will tempt other contries to join, and at the same time increase world trade.

The recourses can be anything from workers, money, technology or raw materials. This system will both help developing countries and and industrialized countries to grow even more. This will also bring the world together. What if a country was struck by a natural disaster? The countries connection threw "the common world recourse bank" would likely to help the country. And maybe the world was struck by a pandemic? Countries would both help each other and the countries that need the most help. I think that the common recourse world bank would lead to "support between countries", "increase world trade" and "more/greater collaboration". This will eventually lead to a better world.
